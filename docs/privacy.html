Privacy Policy — ATTV

Last updated: June 23, 2026

1. Overview

ATTV respects your privacy. This policy describes what data the application handles when you run it locally or on your own server.

2. Data We Process

DataPurposeStorage
URLs you submitFetch and summarize web contentJob database and job folders
Extracted text & imagesGenerate video scriptsdata/jobs/ on your machine
Generated videos & audioPreview and publishdata/jobs/ on your machine
TikTok OAuth tokensPost to your TikTok accountdata/tiktok_tokens.json

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not run a centralized cloud service by default — ATTV runs on your device or server.
  • We do not access your TikTok account except through scopes you authorize.

4. Third-Party Services

Depending on configuration, ATTV may use TikTok API, Google Gemini, Groq, Microsoft Edge TTS, and target websites you provide URLs for.

5. Local Storage & Security

API keys live in your .env file. TikTok tokens are stored locally. You are responsible for securing your machine.

6. Your Choices

  • Do not connect TikTok if you only want local video generation.
  • Delete data/tiktok_tokens.json to remove stored tokens.
  • Revoke ATTV in TikTok: Settings → Security → Apps and websites.

7. Children's Privacy

ATTV is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
